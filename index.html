Frequently Asked Questions
Is GrammarBanana really free? ▼
Yes, 100% free with no hidden costs. No signup, no credit card, no word limits. We're supported by non-intrusive ads and optional sponsorships.
How accurate is the grammar checker? ▼
Very accurate. We use Claude AI — one of the world's most capable language models — which understands context and nuance, not just grammar rules. It catches errors that rule-based checkers miss.
Can I check grammar in languages other than English? ▼
Yes! The AI supports 30+ languages including Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Hindi, Arabic, and more. Just paste your text — it auto-detects the language.
Is my text private? ▼
Absolutely. Your text is sent to the AI for processing and immediately discarded. We do not store, log or share any text you submit. Your content is always private.
What's the difference between the tone options? ▼
Standard fixes basic errors. Formal makes writing more professional. Casual keeps it conversational and relaxed. Academic is best for essays, research papers and reports — avoids contractions and informal language.
