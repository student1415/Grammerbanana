Frequently Asked Questions
Is GrammarBanana really free? ▼
Yes, 100% free with no hidden costs. No signup, no credit card, no word limits — ever. We're supported by non-intrusive ads.
Is this better than Grammarly's free version? ▼
Yes. Grammarly free has heavy word limits, forces account creation, and constantly pushes paid upgrades. GrammarBanana has zero limits, zero signup, zero upsells.
Is there a minimum word count? ▼
No minimum at all. Check a single sentence or a 5,000-word essay — completely free. Unlike QuillBot which requires 80+ words.
What are the 6 writing modes? ▼
General, Email, Essay, Social Media, Cover Letter, and Message. Each mode adjusts the AI's corrections to perfectly fit your writing context.
Is my text private? ▼
Completely. Text is sent to AI for processing only and immediately discarded. We never store, log or share anything you submit.
💡 Quick Tip

Use Academic mode for essays and assignments. It removes contractions, uses precise vocabulary, and ensures formal register automatically.
